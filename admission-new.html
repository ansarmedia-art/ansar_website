Frequently Asked Questions

When does admission start? ▼

Admission typically starts in January and continues until the capacity is filled. Early registration is recommended to secure a seat.

What is the admission fee? ▼

The admission fee varies by grade level. Please contact us for specific details about fees for your child's class.

Are scholarships available? ▼

Yes, merit-based and need-based scholarships are available for eligible students. Contact the admission office for application details.

Is transport facility available? ▼

Yes, we provide transport facility covering multiple routes in the city. Transport charges are separate from tuition fees.

What are the school hours? ▼

School hours are 8:30 AM to 3:30 PM for primary classes and 8:30 AM to 4:00 PM for secondary classes.
